Admission FAQs

Common questions about joining FLYHIGH INSTITUTE Academy.

Yes, we conduct a basic aptitude test to assess your logic, numerical ability, and English proficiency for professional pilot training.

We provide comfortable, supervised hostel accommodations for both male and female students with all modern amenities and nutritious meals.

Absolutely. We have tie-ups with leading banks and provide all necessary documentation to help students secure educational loans.
