Quick Support

Common questions about visiting our campus.

How to reach the campus?

Our head office is located in Sector 18, Gurugram, which is easily accessible via the Yellow Line Metro (IFFCO Chowk Station) and NH-48.

Can I book a virtual counseling?

Yes, we offer virtual counseling sessions via Zoom or Google Meet. Please fill out the inquiry form and mention "Virtual Session" in the subject.

Is there parking available?

Yes, dedicated visitor parking is available at our head office for students and their parents.
