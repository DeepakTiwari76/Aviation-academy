WELCOME TO FLYHIGH

Leading the Way in
Aviation Excellence

FLYHIGH INSTITUTE was founded with a single-minded vision: to bridge the gap between aspiring aviation professionals and the global aerospace industry. We understand that the journey to the cockpit or the cabin requires more than just technical knowledge; it requires discipline, character, and world-class mentorship.

Our academy stands as a beacon of excellence in aviation training, offering comprehensive programs that are meticulously designed to meet and exceed international standards. From state-of-the-art flight simulators to a curriculum recognized by leading civil aviation authorities, we provide an environment where excellence is the only standard.

At FLYHIGH, we don't just teach aviation; we live it. Our team of instructors consists of seasoned captains and senior cabin crew members who bring decades of real-world experience to the classroom. When you choose FLYHIGH, you're not just choosing a training center—you're choosing a lifelong partner in your aviation career.

Our Mission

To provide world-class aviation training through cutting-edge technology and expert mentorship, ensuring our students become industry-ready leaders who prioritize safety and excellence.

Our Vision

To become India's #1 aviation training hub, recognized globally for producing highly skilled professionals who shape the future of the aerospace and travel industry.

Our Values

Integrity, Safety, and Excellence are at the core of everything we do. We believe in transparency, continuous learning, and fostering a culture of mutual respect and professional growth.

OUR FOUNDATION

A Vision Born in Noida (2020)

Established in the year 2020 in the heart of Noida, FLYHIGH INSTITUTE was founded during a pivotal time for the global aviation industry. What started as a focused endeavor to provide high-quality pilot and cabin crew training has now grown into one of India’s most trusted aviation academies.

Under the visionary leadership of our owner, Mr. Arun Tiwari, the institute has maintained a steadfast commitment to excellence, integrity, and student success. His passion for aviation and dedication to shaping the next generation of professionals have been the driving force behind our rapid growth and industry recognition.
